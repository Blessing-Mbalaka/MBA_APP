SECTION D: Nomination/Amendment of assessors
D1: Nomination of internal assessor:
An internal assessor from within CBE may be appointed in the case of assessing a technical research project / limited scope dissertation / minor dissertation as long as that person has had no prior involvement with the research project.
D2: Nomination of external assessors:
  • For minor dissertations at master's level, at least two assessors must be appointed, of whom at least one must be external to the University.
  • For full research dissertations at master's level, at least two external assessors should be appointed.
  • For doctoral theses, at least three assessors should be appointed. All assessors must be external to the University, having not had prior involvement with the project that might compromise there objectivity when assessing the thesis. At least one assessor from outside South Africa should be identified.
The curriculum vitae of each assessor should be attached to this form and forwarded to the CBE Higher Degrees Committee via the Faculty Officer for approval.
